About 5STAR Swimming Academy

Our story, mission, and commitment to swimming excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Olympic swimmer Loapi Seleka, 5STAR Swimming Academy began as a single-lane community program with just 12 students. What started as a passion project quickly grew into the region's premier swimming institution.

In our first decade, we've trained over 5,000 swimmers, produced 32 state champions, and helped countless children and adults develop life-saving water safety skills. Our alumni include NCAA athletes, Olympic trial qualifiers, and most importantly, confident, capable swimmers of all ages.
