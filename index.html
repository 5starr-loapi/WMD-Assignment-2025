Our Programs

Learn to Swim

Children are first-assessed to determine a group or station. Strokes are then constructed systematically, with specific goals at each station or group. Regular assessments are conducted and once elements of a station are mastered, swimmers are promoted to the next station. In this way progress at 5STAR SWIMMING ACADEMY is never static.

  • Parent & Child (6mo-3yrs)
  • Preschool (3-5yrs)
  • School Age (6-12yrs)
  • Teen & Adult

High Performance

Our High-Performance coaching focuses on quality training. Divided into 3 squads; entry-level, junior, and senior-elite, our main goal is to train each swimmer efficiently with personalized interaction. Emphasis is given to mental preparedness.

  • Age Group (8-14yrs)
  • Senior Group (15-18yrs)
  • Elite Performance
  • Year-round training

Adults

Our Adult program is designed for adults who would like to learn an array of skills - from overcoming fear of water, to learning and refining strokes or even Masters events and triathalons. Classes are tailor-made to suit individual needs and convenient times including mornings, evenings and weekends.

  • Private Lessons
  • Swim Team Prep
  • Lifeguard Training
  • Masters Swimming

About 5STAR SWIMMING

Winners of the National Trophy for 19 years; the largest contributor to Botswana National teams and Olympic swimmers.

Our very own state-of-the-art indoor facility truly makes DMSS world class. The new Home Of DMSS boasts two heated training pools, a coffee shop, a pro shop, a children's chill zone, strength training deck, admin offices with a 10-seater board room and spacious change rooms.

5STAR Swimming Academy is Botswana's most successful swim school.
