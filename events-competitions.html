Competition Levels

Development Meets

Introductory competitions for new swimmers focusing on skill development and fun.

  • No qualifying times required
  • Focus on personal improvement
  • Positive, supportive environment

Age Group Competitions

Standard competitions grouped by age with more structured events.

  • 8 & under, 9-10, 11-12, 13-14 age groups
  • Some events may have qualifying times
  • Team scoring and awards

Championship Meets

High-level competitions for experienced swimmers.

  • Qualifying times required
  • Regional and national level events
  • Advanced competition format
